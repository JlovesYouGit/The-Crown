SYSTEM OVERVIEW:
================
The Auto-Initialized Hardware Acceleration System automatically configures and
launches CPU/GPU acceleration for quantum communication services at startup.

- Auto-Initialization on Service Startup

- Windows DLL Auto-Configuration

AUTO-STARTUP FILES:
=================
1. auto_launch_accelerated_service.bat
   - Launches service with hardware acceleration
   - Starts in background mode
   - Configures hybrid targeting

2. register_auto_startup.bat
   - Registers service for Windows auto-startup
   - Adds registry entry for automatic launch
   - Requires Administrator privileges

3. unregister_auto_startup.bat
   - Removes auto-startup registry entry
   - Uninstalls automatic launch configuration

REGISTRY CONFIGURATION:
